Recipe #1

PANCAKES

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: Lunch
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2.6% 1 liter
Eggs 4
Flour 0.5 kg
Vegetable oil 2 spoon
Suger 1.5 spoon
Salt 0.5 teaspoon

COOKING PROCESS:
1. In a large bowl beat eggs and sugar.
2. Part of the milk warm, salt and mix thoroughly with beaten eggs and sugar.
3. Gradually add flour to the resulting mixture, stirring constantly so that there are no lumps.
4. Add some soda, salt and oil. Mix.
5. Add the rest of the milk and beat. The dough should be moderately liquid.
6. Heat up the pan.
7. Fry the pancake on both sides until golden brown.

Recipe #2

Pie "Zebra"

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: Lunch, Dinner
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 2.6% 1 liter
Eggs 5
Flour 250-270 gramm
Butter 200 gramm
Suger 240-320 gramm
Soda 0.5 teaspoon
Sour cream 200 m liter

COOKING PROCESS:
1. Beat eggs with sugar until white foam.
2. Add sifted flour, soda (can be extinguished), melted cooled butter, sour cream and mix thoroughly.
3. Divide the dough into two equal parts. In one part add 2 tbsp. spoons of flour. In another 2 tbsp. cocoa spoons.
4. Grease a wide form (26-28 cm) with oil. Pour into the center alternately small portions of different dough. Do not mix!
5. Add the rest of the milk and beat. The dough should be moderately liquid.
6. Look constantly. If the top of the pie is already baked, but the middle is not yet, you should cover the pie with foil..

Recipe #3

Biscuit

  • Time: 60 min
  • Serves: Lunch, Dinner
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Flour 450 gramm
Butter 250 gramm
Suger 200-300 gramm
Baking powder 0.5 teaspoon

COOKING PROCESS:
1. Grind eggs with sugar until white.
2. Add softened butter. (I knead the dough in a food processor.)
3. Sift flour, mix with baking powder and add to eggs with butter. Mix.
4. Then lightly flour the table, put the dough on it and quickly knead until elastic and smooth. Remove the dough for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
5. Remove the dough from the refrigerator, sprinkle a little flour on the table and roll the dough into a layer. Not very thin, otherwise the cookies will be dry.
6. Cut out cookies in various shapes and place on a greased baking sheet.
7. Place in an oven preheated to 180 degrees and bake cookies for 15-20 minutes.
